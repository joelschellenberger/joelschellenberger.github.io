Hi! My name is Joel (I use he/him pronouns). I'm a multidisciplinary designer passionate about building services that help people solve problems. Currently translating research insights into simple and inclusive experiences with Ontario Digital Service.

Formally Educated

Currently serving a co-op term with Ontario Digital Service, I'm working towards an Honours Bachelor of Design in User Experience Design.

Industry Experienced

I've built up years of experience designing impactful products for partners, clients, and companies; I have the real-world experience to step in and get to work.

Feedback Driven

Iterative, human-centred design is the core of what I do; I work collaboratively with stakeholders to build robust solutions with quantifiable impacts.

Committing to accessible, inclusive design on this website

December 1st, 2020

I want to acknowledge that this portfolio website was built on territories traditionally occupied and cared for by Indigenous Peoples. I attended Wilfrid Laurier University's Brantford campus in an area traditionally occupied by Anishinabeg, Haudenosaunee and Neutral peoples. I want to express my gratitude for the opportunity to learn and grow on this land, which continues to be cared for by the First Peoples of Canada. We must continue to care about the land and about the issues facing Indigenous peoples in Canada.
